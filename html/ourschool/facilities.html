SACRED HEART SCHOOL VACANCIES FOR SUBSTITUTE TEACHERS

To apply, follow the google forms link in the text below.



The Sacred Heart School invites qualified teachers in any / all subject areas apply for inclusion on our substitute teacher list. This list is referred to for short term substitute positions that may arise in the course of the school year.

Please note that the SHS will run competitions for longer term substitute positions via national media and/or online advertising. Submission to this list does not include inclusion for other competitions. For further information please contact the school office on 098 25838.

Click here for application form.

Please note: Garda Vetting & Teaching Council Registration are required.
